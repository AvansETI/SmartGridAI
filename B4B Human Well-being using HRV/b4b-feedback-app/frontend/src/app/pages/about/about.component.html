Who is this app for?
This application is for anyone who would like to predict their thermal well-being based on their HRV (Heart Rate Variability).

What is HRV?
Heart rate variability (HRV) measures the fluctuation in the time intervals between adjacent heartbeats. This beat-to-beat interval (R-R interval) variation is measured in milliseconds and can vary depending on several factors. Being able to answer the question 'what is HRV?' can in fact provide important information about overall health.

How does this work?
How does this work? Our application works based on data gathered from a group of students who gathered data such as their thermal comfort, their HRV, and many more. With this data we have created an AI that has analysed the data and is able to predict the thermal comfort of someone. It does this by looking at the data you provide and compares that data with the data of the different students who have filled in their data. Our model works pretty good but is not perfect, therefore our predictions should be taken with a grain of salt. You are always free to do with your prediction as you please.

How is my data used?
This application uses your data to make a prediction on your thermal comfort level. This data is not stored.
